ABOUT ME

A multidisciplinary designer and visual communicator with a strict eye for detail. Analytical and incredibly organized, with knowledge and experience in both the print and digital worlds. Style is adaptable to client needs and each project is a new opportunity to showcase my capabilities. I have a strong DIY ethic instilled in me from growing up in alternative scenes, which drives my passion for learning new techniques and skills.

Currently working to enhance my front-end coding abilities at the wonderful BrainStation in Toronto. I am very excited to see where it takes me.
